Welcome! The Pomodoro Clock will automatically time work sessions and breaks for you, so you can be your best productive self! Gentle tympani drums will sound when it's time to switch between work or break. The default times are 25 minutes for work sessions and 5 minutes for breaks. You can adjust these anywhere from 1 to 60 minutes with the plus () and minus () buttons. Hit the play button () to start, the pause button () if you need to interrupt the glorious pomodoro technique for some inexplicable reason, or the reset button () if you want to reset the app back to square one.
